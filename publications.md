<div class="pub-entry">
  <h3>Parsing Hiberno-English Dictionary Entries: A Hybrid Rule-Based and LLM Approach</h3>
  <div class="pub-venue">Under preparation · Presentation accepted at Digital Humanities Congress 2026, University of Sheffield · Sep 2–3, 2026</div>
  <div class="pub-authors">Rasika Edirisinghe (et al.)</div>
  <div class="pub-meta">
    <span class="badge badge-wip">Work in Progress</span>
    <span class="tag">Digital Humanities</span>
    <span class="tag">NLP</span>
    <span class="tag">Dictionary Parsing</span>
    <span class="tag">LLMs</span>
    <span class="tag">Hiberno-English</span>
  </div>
  <p>A hybrid system combining rule-based parsers and large language models to extract structured entries from Hiberno-English dictionary sources into a 14-field schema. Introduces a proxy evaluation framework, stratum-level agreement analysis, and audit methodology revealing two systematic root causes of LLM failure modes. Paper (17 pp.) under preparation; talk accepted at DHC 2026.</p>
</div>
